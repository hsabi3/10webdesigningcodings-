Selena Gomez, in full Selena Marie Gomez(born July 22, 1992, Grand Prairie, Texas, U.S)

Inspired by her mother, an amateur actress, Gomez tried out for a role on the PBS children's television series Barney & Friends and, as a result, appeared regularly on the program in 2002-04.
